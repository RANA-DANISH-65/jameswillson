About James Wilson

I was born in Cleveland, OH, the eldest of five children. From an early age, I learned the value of hard work. I got my first job at age 14, a paper route. I did that for three years. During high school I also held other jobs including working in a supermarket, cutting grass, and even doing a stint at McDonalds. Throughout high school I was also very involved with sports, playing on the varsity basketball and baseball teams. In my senior year I hit 27 home runs.

I was admitted to the University of Wisconsin where I studied finance. I earned excellent grades and was awarded the Dean's List recognition every semester during my four years there. It was at college that I got interested in financial planning and insurance. I took a course on managing risk, and the things I learned in that course have never left me. After college I decided to enter the life insurance business, which was the best professional decision I've ever made.

In 2010, I married my college sweetheart, Barbara. Over the next few years, we had four children, two boys, and two girls. I'm a very lucky dad and husband.

In 2020, I earned the RICP professional designation. This further enhanced my skills in retirement income planning.

I am still active in sports, playing in a senior softball league and coaching my kids' basketball and softball teams,
